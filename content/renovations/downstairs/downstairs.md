The downstairs was the final area I tackled, after the [Bedroom][1] and it was an absolute ball buster.

I did a load of plastering and plumbing down here. There were radiator pipes just loosely hanging down the walls and over the floor when we moved in, so I boxed all those in, and joined them to copper pipes for any visible parts as I hate those plastic microbore pipes, they just look cheap.

There were gas pipes running up the side of the room in the lounge  too, so I boxed those in and boxed in the gas box with a small door for access. I also boxed in the electrical fuse box in the hallway as that was just out looking ugly as hell too.

We had a damp problem downstairs, so I had to bite the bullet and get some contractors in to tank the entire ground floor, which cost a few grand. After they'd left, I finished off plastering, then put on skirting, dado and picture rail in all the rooms, and finally decorated it all. 

Then at the very end, we had contractors come in and fit Karndead flooring throughout.
