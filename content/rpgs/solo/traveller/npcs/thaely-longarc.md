---
title: "Thaely Longarc"
---

Thaely Longarc is a well known name in the underground black market network on the industrial planet of Louzy. He can be found at shady down port bars most evenings and acts as a fence for stolen goods. He has a good working relationship with [John Strickland][1] who has supplied him with various ill gotten goods to fence over the last couple decades.
