---
title: "Nod"
---

Nod is a Chokari envoy and most trusted representative of the Chokari peoples on Foelen. The Chokari an uplifted Dolphin race, native the planet of Foelen. Nod is one of the few Chokari who travel offworld to meet with the subsector nobility on Jewell in order to bring forth the concerns of the Chokari people. As such, he has met and made friends with [Sumita Bai][1] who was acting as a diplomat on behalf of the Jewell nobility during one of his many visits to the Subsector capital.
