title: "Jacquel Campbutl"
---

Jacquel Campbutl is an old head at the Jewell News Broadcasting Company. She has worked there as an anchor for the best part of 3 decades now and was one of [Jace Windu's][1] colleagues in his short stint at the agency.

They have remained in touch via bi-yearly X-Boat communique where Jace has fed her information about the goings on in the subsector in return for small payments.
