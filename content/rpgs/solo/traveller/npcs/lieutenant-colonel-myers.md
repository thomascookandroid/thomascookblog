---
title: "Leiutenant Colonel Myers"
---

A serving member of the Jewel subsector marines, he was injured during an assault on Esalin.

During his time healing back at medical facilities in Jewell's star town, he met and became friends with [Hana Suzuki][1], they have remained in touch since, sending correspondence via the X-Boat network since they parted ways 12 years ago.

He is currently stationed on Jewell and his role is primarly training new recruits now.
