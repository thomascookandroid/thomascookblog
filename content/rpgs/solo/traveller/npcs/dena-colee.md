---
title: "Dena Colee"
---

A lecturer at the Jewell Institute for Animal Husbandry, decades ago she was well known for proving and documenting the subsectors most advanced techniques relating to animal surgery. However, [Hana Suzuki][1] found flaws in her thesis and published a widely read and well received paper pointing these out and proposing alternative methods which have since become the new orthodoxy.

Dena still works at the Institute, and relunctantly has to teach new students the now scientifically accepted truths of her prior students work.
