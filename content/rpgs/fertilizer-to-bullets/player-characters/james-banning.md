## Background 

In 2019 James was 22 and was working as a 2nd Lieutenant in the US navy in the role of a military analyst. His work involved tracking targets, following leads via satellite footage and remote feeds, predicting cultural shifts and putting data together to facilitate strikes on enemy positions.

His superior, 1st Lieutenant Grace Ayers had it in for him, and shipped him off to Libya with some special operatives in order to recover a diplomat who had been kidnapped by an LNA splinter group. 

During the mission he was under heavy fire, but managed to survive.

A year later, he was catching up with his father Nathaniel, at a cafe in Thailand when some street thugs rolled up on a moped and shot the place to pieces killing his father.

He vowed to find out who did it and why, and to take vengeance. He formed a PMC called [[Black Dog Company]] and aims to grow it's ranks and look for leads for his fathers killers.
