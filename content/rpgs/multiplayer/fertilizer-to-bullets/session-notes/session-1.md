### Notes
- It's been a year since James Bannings dad has died
- He's living in Singapore with some friends
  - His buddy Haze, who was a Navy Seal, has retired and came to help James
  - Sophia, who owns a bar "The Black Flag" where James and Haze drink
    - Urban bar in a four storey building, the bar is in the basement, with an entrance from the alley
    - Not a tourist bar, dimly lit, the kind of place where less sulubrious elements of society meet in a "neutral" environment
- James owns a PMC called the "Black Dog Company"
  - They have a base inside the same building as "The Black Flag" bar
  - They also have a lockup, electronically secured, with some office space inside, at a second location
- Sophia tells James that a scarred man came in and gave her a card for James with a number to call
- Whilst James thinks, Sophia leaves him to serve customers
- James calls up the number on the card and speaks to Oak, who arranges a flight for James team to Yangdon
- James readies the team up and they head out to Singapore International airport
- James speaks to reception who direct him to a private jet
- James and crew load aboard the jet
- They fly over to Yangdon, landing around 11pm
- After unloading from the plane, James speaks to the pilot who calls Agent Oak and tells him that Black Dog have arrived
- Agent Oak arrives in a blacked out military jeep and gets out
- He informs James about the situation in Myanmar, well his side of the story anyway
- James agrees to the mission and the crew load the gear into the jeep and climb on
- They arrive in Naypyidaw the next morning and notice it's a ghost town
- They pull up at a military building, and Oak leads them upstairs through musty corridors to a briefing room
- Oak shows the PCs satellite imagery of the outpost and the team plan out the operation
- Oak offers to assist James with some men from the Junta, which James accepts
  - James plans to split the squad into 4 fireteams, one team for each squad member
    - Fireteam 1: James
    - Fireteam 2: Hayes, Junta 1
    - Fireteam 3: Martinez, Yen
    - Fireteam 4: Sofia, Junta 2
  - He splits up the map into zones; Z1: Armory and North Generator, Z2: South East Hut, Z3: West Huts, Z4: Mess Hall.
  - He marks up 2 objectives: Obj 1: South Generator, Obj 2: Barracks
  - The plan:
    - Reconnoiter the base for a day ahead of attacking; with fireteam 1 & 2 taking post on the hill to the north of the outpost, and fireteams 3 & 4 taking post in the jungle to the south of the outpost
    - On the day of the attack; fireteams 1 & 2 will secure Obj 2 and then push into Z1 and then into Z4, fireteams 3 & 4 will secure Obj 1 then swing across to secure Z2 and liase with Fireteam 1 at Z4, finally both fireteams will fork and secure Z3
