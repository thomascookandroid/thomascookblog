I got into fishing properly about 5 odd years ago when I asked an old friend, Vas, if he wanted to try out shore fishing (as he was big on fishing back in York on the Ouse).

We ended up getting quite into it going several times a year for a few years, until he moved back to his home country to be with his mum who is getting on now.

Anyway, I took some pictures over the years, and these folders collate those pictures by area with some notes/thoughts from myself about the memories.
